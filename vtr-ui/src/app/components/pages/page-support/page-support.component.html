Low power licence-exempt devices
Operation is subject to the following two conditions: When you use a wireless LAN module in the 802.11 a/n transmission mode, note the following. The devices for the band 5150–5350 MHz are only for indoor usage to reduce potential for harmful interference to co-channel Mobile Satellite systems. High power radars are allocated as primary users (meaning they have priority) of 5250–5350 MHz and 5650–5850 MHz and these radars could cause interference and/or damage to LE-LAN (Licence-Exempt Local Area Network) devices. The maximum antenna gain permitted for devices in the 5250-5350 MHz, 5470-5725 MHz, and 5725-5825 MHz bands complies with the e.i.r.p.
