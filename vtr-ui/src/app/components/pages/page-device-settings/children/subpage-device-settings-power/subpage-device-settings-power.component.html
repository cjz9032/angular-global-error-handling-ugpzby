Intelligent Cooling

This feature dynamically adjusts thermal settings to your needs. When on, it adjusts settings to keep the device cooler or quiet, while being held or idle. Otherwise, it optimizes performance while the device is on a stable surface. When off, you can manually set thermal priority.
